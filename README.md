# EMPOWER ENGINEERS OF NEW JERSEY — WEBSITE

## OVERVIEW

This is the official website for Empower Engineers of New Jersey, a student-led initiative focused on making engineering education accessible, engaging, and hands-on for students from elementary through high school.

The site is designed as a single-page scrolling website to clearly present our mission, team, programs, and opportunities to get involved.

## PURPOSE

Many students lack access to structured engineering education and mentorship. This website serves as a central hub to:

* Share our mission and impact
* Showcase our programs and initiatives
* Recruit volunteers and sponsors
* Provide a point of contact for inquiries

## FEATURES

* Clean, responsive single-page layout
* Sections for About, Programs, Growth, Involvement, Events, Team, and Contact
* Smooth scrolling navigation
* Call-to-action buttons for engagement
* Mobile-friendly design
* Simple contact via email (no form)

## SECTIONS BREAKDOWN

* Hero Section: Organization name, mission tagline, and call-to-action buttons
* About: Detailed mission and vision
* Team: Leadership members and roles
* Programs: Workshops, seminars, projects, and camps
* Growing: Current development and expansion
* Get Involved: Volunteer and sponsorship opportunities
* Contact: Direct email for communication
